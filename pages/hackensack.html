Current Zoning Status & Municipal Ordinances

Hackensack permits cannabis businesses only within designated Cannabis Overlay Zones established by municipal ordinance. Operations must comply with local buffer requirements and all standard zoning rules. Refer to the city's municipal code for complete details.

Licensing Steps Checklist

  1. Verify the property lies within a Cannabis Overlay Zone.
  2. Review Hackensack's cannabis ordinance and zoning buffers.
  3. Submit a conditional-use application to the Planning Board.
  4. Obtain a municipal cannabis license from the city.
  5. Apply for state licensure with the New Jersey Cannabis Regulatory Commission.

Conditional-use Proposals & Updates

The Planning Board reviews conditional-use proposals for cannabis facilities. Meeting agendas and decisions are posted on the city's official website, where the latest updates and public notices are available.
